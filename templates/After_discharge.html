Effective discharge planning can decrease the chances of you readmitting to the hospital, and can also help in recovery, ensure medications are prescribed and given correctly, and adequately prepare you to take care of yourself.







Please check that you have the currently medication:

  • 20 packets - Abilify
  • 15 bottle - Nexium
  • 20 syringe - Humira
  • 30 packets - Crestor

How do I get more supplies of my medicines ?

Take your discharge letter to your GP as soon as possible. They will update your records and give you a prescription before you run out of the medicines you were given when you left hospital.

Where should I store my medicines ?

Always store them out of reach and sight of children, in a cool dark place.
